The core idea of my thesis is to recognise connections between planning and operations research, and use these connections to define new planning algorithms. It covers [CG-iLAO*](/cgilao/), [CoGNe(Re)](/cognere/), [CARL](/carl/), and [i2-dual](/det-pi-for-cssp/).
